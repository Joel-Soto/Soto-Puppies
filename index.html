About Soto Puppies

We’re a small, close-knit family of devoted dog lovers who treat every Siberian Husky we raise like one of our own. Our approach is rooted in trust, compassion, and a deep commitment to raising healthy, well-socialized Huskies that are ready to become cherished members of your family. Recently, our beautiful Siberian Husky gave birth to two wonderful pups: Gina, a lovely brown-coated girl, and Rocky, her playful black-coated brother. Each pup has received proper vet care, early socialization, and lots of affection to ensure they’re ready for lifelong companionship. If you’re interested in welcoming Gina or Rocky into your home, we’d love to hear from you.
